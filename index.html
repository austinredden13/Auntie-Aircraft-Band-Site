Who We Are

Not Quite Country.
Not Quite Anything.

In older, much simpler times, there was never a shortage of songs written about the boys being back in town. Sometime between then and now, we seemingly lost our way.

Auntie Aircraft is a brand new garage rock revival revival band from Nashville, TN — and we've vowed to right this wrong. Because even cowboys need to let loose sometimes.

We wear the hats. We play the guitars. We are emphatically not a country band. You'll figure it out when the amp hits you.
